Portfolio of Rides

Irvine Ondrey Engineering is a small company based in Ann Arbor, Michigan that specializes in the development of electrical control systems for roller coasters and amusement attractions. Brian Ondrey, an alum of Carnegie Mellon Electrical & Computer Engineering, is the president and lead engineer for the company. His experience in the attractions industry dates back to 1998. As an engineering intern, Donovan was able to work and study under Brian and his talened engineering team in the completion of a diverse set of projects.
